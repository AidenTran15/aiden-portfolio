Building Material Business Website

Project Overview

This project is designed to help a building material business in Vietnam attract more customers and effectively market their products. The website provides a user-friendly interface for customers to explore and purchase building materials, enhancing the business's online presence and sales. Key features include an intuitive product catalog, a streamlined contact form, and advanced search filters to improve user experience.

Technologies used

  • Elementor: For creating visually appealing and responsive page layouts.
  • Crocoblock: For adding dynamic content and enhancing functionality.
  • Ninja Form: For creating custom forms to capture leads and inquiries.
  • Jet Engine: For managing custom post types, taxonomies, and meta fields.
  • Smart Filter: For implementing advanced filtering options for product searches.

How do I complete this?

To complete this project thoroughly, I broke it down into several stages. First, I generated the idea, then considered the appropriate technologies, including using Figma for UX/UI design. Finally, I launched the project and tested it to make sure everything was working well

  • Idea Generate

    The idea was to create a modern and efficient website to boost the business's online visibility and customer engagement. The focus was on making it easy for customers to find and purchase building materials while providing the business with a robust platform for marketing their products.

  • Tool and Technologies

    I chose to work with Elementor, Crocoblock, Ninja Form, Jet Engine, and Smart Filter because they offer flexibility, scalability, and ease of use. These tools allowed me to create a professional and functional website tailored to the business's needs.

  • Launced Project

    The website was launched on a reliable hosting service, ensuring it is secure, fast, and capable of handling a high volume of traffic. This allows the business to focus on growth without worrying about website performance issues.

Project result

TThe project resulted in a highly functional and user-friendly website. Customers can easily navigate through the product catalog, use advanced filters to find specific materials, and submit inquiries through custom forms. The integration of Elementor and Crocoblock ensures that the website is visually appealing and dynamic, while Ninja Form and Smart Filter enhance user interaction and search functionality.

Learning and New Experiences

Through this project, I gained practical experience with several WordPress tools and plugins, including:

  • Elementor for creating custom layouts and responsive designs
  • Crocoblock for enhancing website functionality with dynamic content
  • Ninja Form for building and integrating custom forms.
  • Jet Engine for managing complex data structures with custom post types and meta fields.
  • Smart Filter for implementing advanced search and filtering capabilities.
